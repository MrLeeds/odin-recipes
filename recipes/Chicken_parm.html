Chicken Parmesan

This is the new and improved Chicken Parmesan of the 21st century! Take a ride on the wild side and dive into this dish!

Chicken Parm is a staple of the Leeds household, by substituting the Mozzarella for Ricotta and cheddar cheese takes you to places only Mozzaerlla could dream of!

Ingredients for the Chicken

  • 2 large skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ¾ cup dry bread crumbs
  • ½ cup light olive oil for frying, or as needed

Ingredients for the Cheese Spread

  • ½ cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup shredded sharp white Cheddar cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese for topping
